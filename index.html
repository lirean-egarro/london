Lirean is a London-based company dedicated to creating smart, intuitive tools that revolutionise language learning. We draw from research in brain sciences and linguistics to make learning fast, fun and long-lasting.

Learning should be research-based:

 

Our team of speech scientists from University College London translates over 30 years of academic research  into practical language learning tools which are scientifically proven to work.

 

Learning should be fun:

 

Motivation is the foundation of all learning.

Our gamification strategies make learning fun so that you can relax and learn a language at the same time.

You’re never too busy to learn:

 

Our mobile apps will bring you learning that fits into your schedule so you can learn a language on the subway, whilst waiting in line – wherever and whenever you choose!

With our combined expertise in linguistics, brain sciences, foreign language teaching, game design and software engineering, we’re bringing language learning to the next level.
